Our product is a database of recipes of dishes. Users can view recipes of dishes, add favorites to themselves, also share their recipes and write a comment. Our goal is to unite people who like cooking, share their secrets and knowledges, and learn how to cook.

Some people think that the main thing is to eat something despite the appearance and the way of cooking. We disagree with this. Cooks delicious and beautifully it's always nice, one dish can lift a person's mood for the whole day, make them happy, and feel like a real chef.
